Past Members of the Acute Exposure Guideline Levels (AEGL) Committee (1996-2011)

The last meeting of the National Advisory Committee for Acute Exposure Guideline Levels for Hazardous Substances (NAC/AEGL Committee) was in April 2010, and its charter expired in October 2011. Although the work of the NAC/AEGL Committee ended, the AEGL program continues to operate at EPA and works with the National Academies to publish final AEGLs.
