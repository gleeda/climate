Acute Exposure Guideline Levels (AEGLs) Committee Meetings and Minutes (1996-2010)

The National Advisory Committee for the Development of Acute Exposure Guideline Levels (AEGLs) for Hazardous Substances (AEGL Committee) was formed in June 1996 to develop and recommend AEGLs for hazardous chemicals for use in chemical emergency programs.

On this page, you can link to information from past AEGL Committee meetings, Federal Register (FR) meeting notices and minutes. These pages will provide information about meeting times, locations and chemicals considered. The minutes provide rationales for the AEGL values and voting data.
