Funding History for Brownfields and Land Revitalization in New England

EPA's Brownfields Programs provide funding and technical assistance to local, tribal, and state entities to assess, safely clean, and sustainably reuse brownfields. Programs address the environmental, health, and economic concerns associated with brownfields.

The links on the right access tables and maps displaying all grantees in New England that have received Brownfields funding to date by state and program.
