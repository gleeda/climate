Region 6: Children's Environmental U.S.-MX Border Symposium (2015)

Children's Environmental Health on the Border

Symposium Information

Texas Tech University Health Sciences Center - El Paso
El Paso, Texas

Dates: September 24-25, 2015
Cost: Free 

 

Protecting Children Where they Live, Learn and Play

The symposium provided important information regarding the link between the impacts of the environment on children’s health. Topical sessions were held for health care providers, academics, community health workers, policy makers, social workers and community leaders. ​

A summary document of the symposium can be found below.


For additional information regarding symposium, contact:

Paula Selzer, selzer.paula@epa.gov
