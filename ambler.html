Environmental Concerns of Communities around Ambler, Pennsylvania

Environmental concerns around Ambler, Pennsylvania include two Superfund sites: BoRit Asbestos Superfund Site and Ambler Asbestos Piles Superfund Site, both of which are primarily contaminated with asbestos-containing waste materials. EPA works with its partners to address contamination in and around communities in Ambler, Pennsylvania.
