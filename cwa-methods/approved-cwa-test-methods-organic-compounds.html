Approved CWA Test Methods: Organic Compounds

Methods approved under Clean Water Act section 304(h) and published at 40 CFR Part 136.
 
Note: The drinking water method 525.1 is approved for Clean Water Act use, but it is not approved for Safe Drinking Water Act compliance monitoring.
