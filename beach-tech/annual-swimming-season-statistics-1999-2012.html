Annual Swimming Season Statistics (1999-2012)

Summary of Annual Beach Notifications

You will need Adobe Reader to view some of the files on this page. See EPAâ€™s About PDF page to learn more.

To help beachgoers make informed decisions about swimming at U.S. beaches, EPA gathers state-by-state data about beach closings and advisories. Between 1999 and 2012, EPA published a national summary report about the previous yearâ€™s swimming season data.

You will need Excel Viewer to view some of the files on this page. See Microsoft Excel page to learn more.

The 2012 report is the final annual beach report. You can find the most currently available swimming season statistics in BEACON.
