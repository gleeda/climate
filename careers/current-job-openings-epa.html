Current Job Openings at EPA

It takes people with many different backgrounds and skill sets to address the multifaceted challenges EPA faces every day. Whether you are enforcing environmental regulations or conducting research, at EPA, you can leverage your specialized job skills into meaningful work for your community, your state and your nation.

Our vacancy announcements are posted on USAJobs.gov, the official job site of the United States federal government.

Using Advanced Search on USAJobs.gov to Narrow Your Search

You can use the main USAJobs.gov search page to find current openings.  In addition, you can tailor your search on USAJobs by using the Advanced Search feature:

  • Go to USAJobs's Advanced Search page (link will open in a new tab/window).  You will see all the ways that you can narrow your search.
  • To view only U.S. EPA jobs,
    • Click on the arrow to the left of "Department and Agency." 
    • Next to "Show Available Jobs in", type "Environmental Protection Agency". 
    • You will see a drop-down menu with one item in it.  Click on that item. 
    • Make sure that EPA appears in the "You Have Added" box.
  • If you want to further narrow your search at this point, select specific locations, salary ranges, etc.
  • Then click on "Search Jobs" at the bottom of the page.
