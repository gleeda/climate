Additional Resources for Citizen Science - Environmental Protection

You will need Adobe Reader to view some of the files on this page. See EPA’s About PDF page to learn more.

General

  • MyEnvironment: Gathers data from EPA and other resources by your area code – includes MyWater, MyAir, MyHealth, MyEnergy and MyCommunity
  • Green Apps: Over 290 apps to help you understand and protect the environment
  • Grants.gov: An overview of the process to apply for federal grants
  • National Environmental Methods Index (NEMI): Searchable collection of environmental analysis methods

Recent Workshops and Webinars

  • Community Air Monitoring Training - July 9, 2015
  • Protecting the Anacostia Watershed – A Workshop on Water Quality Standards

Air

  • Air Sensor Science and Education: Slideshow on empowering students, citizen scientists, and communities using air sensor science
  • EPA Basic Air Information Page: Introduction to air pollution
  • EPA Hazardous Air Pollutants Page: Basic introduction to hazardous air pollutants
  • EPA Quality Assurance Guidance Documents for Air Monitoring
  • AirNow: Provides the public with easy access to national air quality information 

Water

  • EPA Headquarters Water Page: Information on everything water
  • EPA Watershed Academy: Training and information on implementing watershed approaches
  • Biological Water Quality Criteria Page: Information about the health of aquatic ecosystems
  • National Estuary Program: A network of voluntary community-based programs that safeguard the health of coastal ecosystems 
  • Volunteer Wetland Monitoring: An Introduction and Resource Guide: Introduction to volunteer wetland monitoring, including handbooks and manuals
  • National Water Quality Monitoring Council: A forum to improve the Nation's water quality through partnerships and increased understanding and stewardship 
  • EPA Regional Equipment Loan Programs: Lending programs assist communities with citizen science monitoring projects. Currently, programs operate in US EPA Region 1 and Region 2 for water monitoring only. Please contact us for more information.

Soil

  • Brownfields and Urban Agriculture: Interim Guidelines for Safe Gardening Practices (PDF): Outlining the range of issues to be addressed to safely grow food on former Brownfields sites.

Quality Assurance

  • EPA's Quality System for Environmental Data and Technology: Ensuring that environmental data are of sufficient quantity and quality to support the data's intended use. The system also applies to grantees, and other recipients of financial assistance from EPA
  • Quality Assurance Project Plan template for Citizen Science Projects (PDF)(24 pp, 211 K)
