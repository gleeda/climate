Rule Summary

In 2011, based on the Environmental Protection Agency's (EPA) review of the air quality criteria for CO, EPA issued a decision to retain the existing NAAQS for CO.

Rule History

08/31/2011 – Final Decision to Retain the NAAQS for CO

02/11/2011 – Proposed Decision to Retain the NAAQS for CO
