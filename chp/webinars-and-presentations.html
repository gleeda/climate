  • How Combined Heat and Power Saves Money, Reduces Emissions and Improves Energy Security, Environmental and Energy Study Institute (EESI) Briefing (Presentation)
    May 22, 2013
     

    On May 22, the EPA Combined Heat and Power Partnership (CHPP) presented at the Environmental and Energy Study Institute (EESI) briefing: How Combined Heat and Power Saves Money, Reduces Emissions and Improves Energy Security. The briefing discussed how CHP technology can provide critical facilities (e.g. hospitals, wastewater treatment), businesses, institutions, and communities with more resilient and reliable heat and power, while at the same time reducing energy costs and harmful emissions over time. The CHPP presentation focused on EPA and other federal efforts to promote the environmental, economic, and resiliency benefits of CHP.

    Presentation:
    • All presentations (including Susan Wickwire, U.S. EPA) Exit
