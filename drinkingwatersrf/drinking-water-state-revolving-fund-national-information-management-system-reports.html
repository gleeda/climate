Drinking Water State Revolving Fund National Information Management System Reports

These DWSRF reports provide specific financial information for the individual states and territories.

The financial data includes, among other information:

  • Grants received
  • Leveraged funds
  • Loan funds provided
  • Set-aside funds assistance
  • Financial assistance by water system size
You will need Adobe Reader to view some of the files on this page. See EPA’s About PDF page to learn more.
  • Drinking Water SRF Program Information National Summary, November 2016 (PDF)(26 pp, 262 K, 2016)
    This national summary provides specific financial information about the DWSRF program for all states and Puerto Rico.
  • Data Elements for the DWSRF Information System (PDF)(10 pp, 210 K)
    The definitions of data field names in the national and state financial reports of the DWSRF program.
  • State and National Roll-up Reports 2016 (PDF)(164 pp, 2 MB, 2016)
    Combined document of 2016's State and National roll-up reports.
