Reports and Fact Sheets about the Drinking Water State Revolving Fund (DWSRF)

These fact sheets describe various aspects of the DWSRF program.

  • 2009 DWSRF Annual Report (PDF)(EPA 816-r-10-021)
    Report on the financial performance of the DWSRF during 2009, including ARRA. Also include projection of fund performance in future years.
  • Drinking Water State Revolving Fund: Investing in a Sustainable Future( EPA 816-r-08-002)
    This report highlights the 10th anniversary of the DWSR.
  • Drinking Water State Revolving Fund: Increasing Impact(EPA 816-r-07-002)
    This report highlights the accomplishments and financial position of this exceptional program.
  • DWSRF Program Overview (EPA 816-F-00-028)
    A general description of the Drinking Water State Revolving Fund program.
  • Public Participation in the DWSRF Program(EPA 816-F-00-015)
    A description of steps that concerned citizens can take to give input to the DWSRF program.
  • Using DWSRF Set-Aside Funds to Assist Small Water Systems (EPA 816-F-00-014)
    An explanation of DWSRF funds for technical assistance to small water systems.
  • Using DWSRF Set-Aside Funds for Source Water Protection (EPA 816-F-00-013)
    An explanation of DWSRF funds that states may use to protect source waters.
  • Using DWSRF Set-Aside Funds for Operator Certification (EPA 816-F-00-012)
    An explanation of DWSRF funds that states may use to support certification of water system operators.
  • Using DWSRF Set-Aside Funds for Capacity Development (EPA 816-F-00-011)
    An explanation of DWSRF funds that states may use to support technical, managerial and financial capacity of water systems.
  • Funding Methyl Tertiary Butyl Ether (MTBE) Prevention and Remediation Projects with the Clean Water State Revolving Fund (CWSRF) (EPA 832-F-00-004)
    An explanation of CWSRF funds that states may use to address MTBE at sites of concern.
  • Funding Class V Injection Well Closures with the Clean Water State Revolving Fund (CWSRF) (EPA 832-F-00-074)
    An explanation of CWSRF funds that states may use to close shallow injection wells.
  • Using the DWSRF for Source Water Protection Loans (EPA 816-F-01-039)
    An explanation of DWSRF funds that states may use to protect sources of drinking water.
  • Using the DWSRF to Implement Security Measures at Public Water Systems (EPA 816-F-02-040)
    An explanation of DWSRF funds that states may use to provide security at water systems.
  • Using DWSRF Funds to Comply with the New Arsenic Rule (EPA 816-F-02-004)
    An explanation of DWSRF funds use at water systems to comply with the arsenic regulation.
  • Using DWSRF Funds to Comply with the Radionuclides Rule (EPA 816-F-02-008)
    An explanation of DWSRF funds use at water systems to comply with the radionuclides regulation.
  • Using DWSRF Funds to Comply with the Stage 1 Disinfectants and Disinfection Byproducts Rule (EPA 816-F-02-007)
    An explanation of DWSRF funds use at water systems to comply with regulations setting standards for disinfectants and disinfection byproducts in drinking water.
  • Using DWSRF Funds to Comply with the Filter Backwash Recycling Rule (EPA 816-F-02-006)
    An explanation of DWSRF funds use at water systems to comply with regulations on filter backwash recycling at water systems.
  • Using DWSRF Funds to Comply with the Long Term 1 Enhanced Surface Water Treatment Rule (EPA 816-F-02-005)
    An explanation of DWSRF funds use at water systems to comply with regulations on surface water treatment.
  • Using DWSRF Funds for Transmission and Distribution Needs (EPA 816-F-03-003)
    An explanation of DWSRF funds use at water systems to finance transmission and distribution pipe.
  • Funding Water Efficiency Through the State Revolving Fund Programs (EPA 816-F-03-022)
    An explanation of clean water and drinking water SRF program funding for water efficiency projects and activities.
  • Implementing Area-Wide Optimization Programs (AWOPs) through the Capacity Development and the DWSRF Programs (EPA 816-F-03-019)
    An explanation of DWSRF funds that states may use to support area-wide optimization programs at water systems.
  • Funding for Source Water Protection Activities (EPA 816-K-03-004)
    An explanation of DWSRF funds that states may use for source water protection activities.
  • DWSRF Green Project Reserve Example Business Cases (EPA 816-K-11-001)
    Examples demonstrating documentation of green projects funded through the DWSRF.
