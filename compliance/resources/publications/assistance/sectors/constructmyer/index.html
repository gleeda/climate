Managing Your Environmental Responsibilities: A Planning Guide for Construction and Development

You will need Adobe Reader to view some of the files on this page. See EPA's PDF page to learn more.

Managing Your Environmental Responsibilities: A Planning Guide for Construction and Development (PDF) (MYER) is a guidance manual that provides the construction industry comprehensive environmental information. (255 pp, 2386K)

Printed copies of this document are available free of charge from EPA's document warehouse, the National Service Center for Environmental Publications. Please specify EPA document number 305-B-04-003.

There are two parts to this guide.

Forward and Disclaimer (PDF) (3 pp, 134K)

  • Part I
    • a series of questions to facilitate the assignment of environmental responsibilities
    • detailed regulatory background
    • case studies
  • Part II
    • self-audit checklists

The owner, contractor, architect, and other personnel involving in construction and development can use this guide during the pre-bid phase to learn what is required of them and factor in the cost for compliance. They should also use this document during the pre-construction phase to assign environmental responsibilities, and during the construction phase to find answers to ongoing environmental questions or conducting self audits.
