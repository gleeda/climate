Audit Policy eDisclosure and Regional Contacts for New Owner Disclosures

Reporting a Self-Disclosure under eDisclosure

Once a violation has been discovered, a company has 21 days from the time of that discovery to disclose in writing the violation to EPA. The disclosure should be made through EPA’s eDisclosure Portal. See eDisclosure webpage.

Regional Contacts for Making New Owner Disclosures

Disclosures under the New Owner Policy should be faxed or mailed to the EPA Region in which the entity or facility is located. For potential criminal violations or civil violations in multiple EPA Regions, the disclosure should be made to EPA Headquarters.
