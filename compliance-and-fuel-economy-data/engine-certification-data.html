Engine Certification Data

This page provides certification data from various engine manufacturers for past and current model years.

NOTE:

Beginning in Model Year 2011, the data for several industries have migrated to the Verify Database system. Therefore, a difference in formatting may be noticed in the resulting files. Most industries that migrate to Verify will no longer have FileMaker Pro files included.

On this page, there is certification data for:
  • Large engines
    (including nonroad compression-ignition (NRCI) and on-highway heavy-duty diesel and gasoline)
  • Small nonroad spark-ignition (NRSI) engines
  • Marine spark-ignition (SI) engines
  • Marine large compression-ignition (CI) engines
  • Snowmobile SI engines
  • Locomotive CI engines
  • Large NRSI engines
  • Nonroad evaporative components
  • Heavy-duty evaporative components
  • Heavy-duty vehicles
