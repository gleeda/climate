Before contacting the EPA about your question, please review the information provided on the Clean Air Act site and provided on this page.

Is your question about…

  • general environmental or health (including noise) concerns? Local and state governments often have ordinances or regulations that address health concerns and many environmental programs have been delegated to other federal, tribal, state or local agencies and they have primary responsibility for them. As a result, it is often, it is most useful to contact your local (city or county) or state environmental or health agency first. Additionally, our EPA Regional Office Emergency Response Staff may be able to assist you in determining how any environmental or health risks are being addressed.  You can find contact information on our community emergency response page.
  • getting agency records?  Copies of most agency records must be obtained through the FOIA process; please do not request agency records using this form.
    • More about FOIA
    • Which records are available without a FOIA request
  • commenting or reading comments on a proposed rule? If so, go directly to regulations.gov, and follow the online instructions for submitting comments.

Didn't find the answer you were looking for? Check to see if your question is in the agency's FAQ database, or one of the below topic specific FAQ databases.

  • Air Now
  • Climate Change
  • ENERGY STAR
  • Transportation Fuels
  • Indoor Air Quality
  • Radiation

To submit questions or comments about the Clean Air Act, or this website, please use the form below or send mail to the address below. Be sure to include your e-mail address if you’d like a response.

Please bear in mind that we cannot provide answers to certain types of requests (e.g., commercial endorsements). Comments on specific EPA programs will be forwarded to the responsible office within the Agency. While we strive to respond to every question submitted with an answer or an appropriate referral as quickly as possible, responses can take some time.
