Clean Water State Revolving Fund (CWSRF) National Information Management System Reports

EPA collects data annually from the state CWSRF programs to document program progress and account for the use of federal funds. Data collected from the state CWSRF programs are stored in the CWSRF National Information Management System (NIMS).

Reports provide national, regional, and state-by-state information on the sources of funds available to the CWSRF programs and the way these funds are used for a range of water quality protection projects.
