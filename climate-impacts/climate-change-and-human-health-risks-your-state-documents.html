Climate Change and Human Health Risks in Your State - Documents

Select a state below to learn about climate change health impacts, related public health concerns, and how you can take action to prepare for or respond to climate change. Key resources for each state are also provided. Information about impacts is based on The Impacts of Climate Change on Human Health in the United States: A Scientific Assessment and the National Climate Assessment.

Find additional resources on the Climate Change and Human Health Risks in Your State page. Learn more about other climate change impacts in your state.
