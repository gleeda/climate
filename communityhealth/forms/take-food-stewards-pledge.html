Take EPA’s Food Steward’s Pledge and commit to reduce wasted food and hunger. Read the pledge action steps for more ideas.

After you submit your pledge, you will go to a page to print out your pledge certificate and we will send a Welcome Kit to your email address.

By taking the pledge, I agree to allow EPA to use my personal information in connection with my participation in the Food Steward's Pledge. To learn how EPA handles your personal data see the Privacy Notice.
