Scheduled Date of NODA Type of NODA CASPR NUSA Program(s) Covered Description
Schedule of Cross-State Air Pollution Rule (CSAPR) New Unit Set Aside (NUSA) Notices of Data Availbility (NODAs) by "Round"
June 1 Round 1 Preliminary NODA CSAPR Ozone Season (OS), Nitrogen Oxide (NOx) Annual, Sulfur Dioxide (SO2) Group1, and SO2 Group 2 Preliminary allowance allocation calculations are based on a unit's emissions in tons in the previous calendar year.
August 1 Round 1 Final NODA CSAPR OS, NOx Annual, SO2 Group 1, and SO2 Group 2 August 1st is also the deadline by which U.S. EPA must record the round 1 allowance allocations.
September 15 Round 2 Preliminary NODA CSAPR OS Second round preliminary NODAs list units EPA believes are eligible for a 2nd round NUSA allocation; no emissions data or allowance calculations are provided.
November 15 Round 2 Final NODA CSAPR OS Round 2 allocations for the CSAPR OS NUSA are based on a unit's ozone season emissions in tons as of September 30 of the current calendar year. November 15 is also the deadline by which U.S. EPA must record the round 2 allowance allocations.
December 15 Round 2 Preliminary NODA CSAPR NOx Annual, SO2 Group 1, and SO2 Group 2 Second round preliminary NODAs list units EPA believes are eligible for a 2nd round allocation; no emissions data or allowance calculations are provided.
February 15 Round 2 Final NODA CSAPR NOx Annual, SO2 Group 1, and SO2 Group 2 Round 2 allocations for the CSAPR NOx Annual, SO2 Group 1, and SO2 Group 2 NUSA are based on a unit's annual emissions in tons as of December 31 of the previous calendar year. February 15 is also the deadline by which U.S. EPA must record the round 2 allowance allocations.
