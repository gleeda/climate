Join the Network

Joining the Coalbed Methane Outreach Program (CMOP) Network is free and voluntary. As a CMOP Network Member, you:

  • Will automatically receive our periodic news announcements, the CMM Monthly News, via email.
  • Can add your organization and your contact information to our Industry Contacts list, which is featured on the CMOP Web site and to which we direct questions about the industry

To join, please fill out the information below and select "Submit."

Contact Information
Please provide a link to the specific CMM and/or CBM page on your company's website, if possible.
How would you categorize your organization?
If yes, your company name, website, organization type, specialty, and location will be publicly displayed on the CMOP website Industry Contacts page. Your name and email address will be listed as contact information. You must be a direct employee of the company to qualify as the contact. Only one entry per company will be displayed. Please do not use abbreviations or acronyms. It might take up to one month for the CMOP website to reflect your request - possibly longer, if your request requires additional verification. PLEASE NOTE: LISTING IN THIS DIRECTORY DOES NOT IMPLY ENDORSEMENT BY THE U.S. EPA.
