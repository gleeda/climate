Superfund Analytical Services and Contract Laboratory Program

The Analytical Services Branch (ASB) strives to be a premier provider of analytical services for the Superfund Remedial Program and other EPA programs. ASB manages the Contract Laboratory Program (CLP) which provides ready access to analytical services via a wide spectrum of private contracted environmental laboratories and associated data review and assessment tools.
