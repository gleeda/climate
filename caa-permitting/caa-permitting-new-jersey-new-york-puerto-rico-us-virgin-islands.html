State/Tribal/Local Permitting Authorities

Region 2 has approved or delegated authority for these states and other jurisdictions to issue certain CAA permits.
The region retains responsibility for all air-related permitting on tribal lands and carries out oversight on the state and local programs. To learn more, select the agency of interest below.
