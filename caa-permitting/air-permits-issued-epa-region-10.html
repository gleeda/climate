Types of permits issued by Region 10

  • PSD - Prevention of Significant Deterioration major source construction permits in "attainment areas." Note: EPA co-issues PSD permits under the jurisdiction of the Washington Energy Facility Site Evaluation Council (EFSEC). See Projects Under EFSEC Jurisdiction Exit
  • nNSR – Major source construction permits in "nonattainment areas"
  • mNSR – Minor source construction permits in Indian Country
  • Title V – Major source Part 71 operating permits (only in Indian Country in ID, OR, WA)
  • Non-Title V – Federal Air Rules for Reservations (FARR) operating permits (only in Indian Country in ID, OR, WA)
  • OCS – Outer Continental Shelf construction and operating permits (except off the north slope of Alaska)

Approved Requests for Coverage Under a General Permit or Permit by Rule

Source Categories

General permits or permits by rule are now available at EPA's Tribal Minor New Source Review web page for the following source categories:

  • Hot mix asphalt (HMA) plants
  • Stone quarrying, crushing, and screening (SQCS) facilities
  • Gasoline dispensing facilities
  • Petroleum dry cleaners
  • Auto body repair and miscellaneous surface coating operations
  • Concrete batch plants
  • Boilers and emergency engines
  • Stationary spark ignition engines
  • Stationary compression ignition engines
  • Graphic arts and printing operations
  • Sawmill facilities
