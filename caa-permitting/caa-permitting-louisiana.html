CAA Permitting in Louisiana

Clean Air Act permitting in Louisiana is the responsibility of the Air Permits Division Exit of the  Louisiana Department of Environmental Quality (LDEQ).

Responsible Area

  • Statewide, except in Indian Country, where EPA Region 6 is the permitting authority
