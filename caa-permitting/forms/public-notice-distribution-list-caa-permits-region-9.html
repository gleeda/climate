Public Notice Distribution List for CAA Permits, Region 9

Members of the public who are interested in receiving notices and other information regarding air permits issued by EPA Region 9 (Arizona, California, Hawaii, Nevada, and tribes within these states) may use this page to join our e-mail distribution lists. To join the list, complete these steps:

  1. Enter your contact information in the form below. 
  2. Indicate which permits you are interested in. 
  3. Click on the Submit button at the bottom of the page. 

To unsubscribe from the list, send an e-mail to R9AirPermits@epa.gov (R9AirPermits@epa.gov). When sending a request to unsubscribe, please use the email you wish to have removed from our distribution list.
