2016 School Bus Replacement and Retrofit Rebates

Over 500 school bus fleets applied to EPA’s 2016 School Bus Rebate Program, requesting over $44 million in funding. Each application was given a unique identification number and using these, applications were randomly selected and placed in order on a list.

Starting at the top of the list, funds were reserved for applicants until all funding allocated by EPA headquarters and Regions were reserved. Over $7.7 million was reserved for 88 applicants. The recipients are listed below. Selections with an asterisk (*) were funded by EPA Regions. Applicants who were not selected were placed on the Applicant Wait List in the random order in which they were picked. While unlikely, applicants on the wait list may be contacted if additional funding becomes available for them.
